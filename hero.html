Developing African Societies Through Health, Education, And Agriculture

IAAF, Ghana is an NGO which aims to develop society and improve deprived communities in Africa in selected areas of Health, Education and Agriculture. It seeks to achieve this through its network (alliance) of Partners, Funders, Consultants, Advisors and dedicated and Passionate Staff.
